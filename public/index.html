Jelly No Puzzle (Remake)

Goal of the game

When "jellies" of the same color touch, they merge together. You goal is to merge all jellies.

How to play?

Left-click to move a piece to the left, right-click to move it to the right.
If you press T, you can toggle unified-click mode. Click on the left side of a cube moves it to the right and vice versa.

You can press Z to undo a move and R to reset the level (which is also undoable!).

Progress is saved on the device, so you can come back later if you're stuck.
