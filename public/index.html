Jelly No Puzzle (Remake)

Goal of the game

When "jellies" of the same color touch, they merge together. You goal is to fully merge all jellies.

How to play?

Click on a piece to move it to the direction of which the arrow is pointing. Jellies can't go through walls and will merge together when next to each other.

Black pieces can't merge. Solid blocks are anchored down and are immovable.

Progress is saved on the device, so you can come back later if you're stuck.

Cheatsheet
  • Z: undo move.
  • R: reset level.
  • G: toggles display of gridlines on/off.
  • I: toggle between direct input (default) or old school click mode (right click is move right).
